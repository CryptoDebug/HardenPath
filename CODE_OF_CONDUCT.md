# Code of Conduct

HardenPath contributors are expected to be respectful, constructive, and safety-minded.

## Expected Behavior

- Treat others with respect.
- Give feedback on ideas and code, not personal identity.
- Keep discussions legal, ethical, and educational.
- Respect privacy and confidentiality.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, or discriminatory language.
- Sharing credentials, private data, or exploit evidence from real unauthorized systems.
- Encouraging attacks against systems without explicit authorization.
- Publishing content that bypasses the ethical scope of the project.

Maintainers may remove content or restrict participation when behavior conflicts with this code.
